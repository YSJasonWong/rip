關於計劃

理念

身後事務所是一個網絡平台, 供使用者在生前交代身後事的地方。若使用者不幸離世, 他生前在此留下的遺言將有辦法傳送到家人及朋友。目前, 使用者可以將部分個人資料存放於我們的資料庫中, 亦可以在此留下一些句子, 讓家人可以遵照他們的意願處理身後事。未來, 身後事務所將會有更完善的服務, 包括定期詢問使用者是否仍然在生、以及為某人留下定制留言。

如何使用

  • 開始留言之前, 請先註冊帳戶。帳戶的個人資料將會紀錄在資料庫中, 方便日後使用及更改。請放心, 用戶密碼經過加密, 我們並不能透過資料庫得知你所輸入的密碼。
  • 開設帳戶後, 你可以選擇一張照片作為自己的遺照。
  • 你亦可在此留下解鎖電話的密碼, 方便家人在你離世後解鎖電話。
  • 你可增設重要聯絡人, 日後平台功能完全運作時, 此重要聯絡人將有存取你的資料的權限, 請務必細心挑選信任的人。
  • 最後, 請告知你的親朋好友關於此網站, 並邀請他們加入成為用戶。

關於開發者:

Jason Wong是城市大學創意媒體學院的應屆畢業生。希望透過此計劃, 讓人明白人生苦短, 及時行樂, 以及讓人思考生命中最重要的價值。此計劃亦鼓勵用戶將心底說話講出口, 勿讓自己留遺憾。

About this project

Our Notion

Funeral Agency is an online platform, for users to publish their last words. If the user has passed away, unfortunately, his/her last words will be sent to family and friends. For now, users are allowed to store some of their information as well as their last words in our database, so that their families can reach and refer to their wishes after their death. In the future, Funeral Agency will be completed with integrated services. E.g. Regularly ask whether users are still alive, and customise messages for independent recipients.

How to use

  • Before you start typing messages, please register an account. Your personal information will be stored in the database where you are allowed to edit again in the future. The password you typed is encrypted, we are not able to access what you have typed for the password.
  • After you registered an account, you can select and upload an image for your posthumous photo.
  • You can save your phone’s passcode, your family can restore your phone if you passed away.
  • You are allowed to set a close contact person. When the platform is fully functioning, the contact you set is authorised to retrieve your data. Therefore, please choose your close contact carefully.
  • Finally, please tell your family and friends about this website, and invite them to join as a member.
